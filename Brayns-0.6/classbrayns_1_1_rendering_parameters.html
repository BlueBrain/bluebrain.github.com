float brayns::RenderingParameters::getEpsilon ( ) const
inline

Raytracers have to deal with the finite precision of computer calculations.

Since the origin of the reflected ray lies on the surface of the object, there will be an intersection point at zero distance. Since we do not want that, all intersection distances less than the epsilon value are ignored.
