Detailed Description

Assemble small frame tiles (e.g.

64x64) into 512x512 ones.

This class operates on all the channels of the frame. The tile indices are global for the frame, i.e. individual channels are mapped to contiguous, non-overlapping sets of indices by computeVisibleSet().
