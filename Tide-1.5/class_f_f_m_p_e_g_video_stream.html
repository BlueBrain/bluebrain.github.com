int64_t FFMPEGVideoStream::decodeTimestamp ( AVPacket &  packet)

Partially decode a video packet to determine its timestamp.

This function is intended to help reach a desired timestamp, for instance after seeking the file, without the need to decode the full picture.

Only ONE of decode or decodeTimestamp must be called for a single packet, otherwise the behaviour of the stream may become undefined (wrong picture timestamp, for example).
