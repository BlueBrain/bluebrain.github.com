typedef std::pair< uint32_t, size_t > brain::SynapseGID

The GID of a synapse is the a tuple of two numbers:

  • The GID of the post-synaptic cell.
  • The index of the synapse in the array of afferent contacts of the post-synaptic cell before pruning/filtering. GIDs are invariant regardless of how the structural touches are converted into functional synapses during circuit building.
